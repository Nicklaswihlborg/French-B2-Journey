Welcome to French B2 Journey

This is your companion to reach the French B2 level. Use the vocabulary section to learn and practice important words and phrases, and use the calendar to track your progress and see how much time is left until your goal.

The site is designed to be clean, minimalistic, and interactive. You can hear the pronunciation of French words with the built-in speech synthesis, and mark your study days on the calendar to keep yourself accountable. Good luck on your language learning journey!
